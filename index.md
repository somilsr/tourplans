## Day 0 – Fri 28 Nov: Hyderabad → Nashik (Train)
<div class="day-content">
- **Train:** Devagiri Express 17058  
- **Departs:** 1:10 PM from Hyderabad Deccan (HYB)  
- **Arrives:** ~3:00 AM Nashik Road Station (NK)  
- **Fare:** ₹500 per person (Sleeper)  
- **Night:** On train  
- **Tips:** Carry snacks, warm clothing, earplugs, power bank
</div>

## Day 1 – Sat 29 Nov: Nashik Sightseeing + Saptashrungi (Bike)
<div class="day-content">
- **3:00 AM:** Arrive Nashik → 15 min cab to hotel  
- **Hotel:** Budget hotel near CBS / Panchavati (₹700–900/night)  
- **7 AM:** Pick up bike (₹700–900/day + ₹2000 deposit)  

**Route & Timings:**

| Time         | Destination                              | Distance                 | Highlights                                   |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| 7 AM → 8 AM  | Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga                | 30 km                    | Kushavart Kund, Godavari origin              |
| 9 AM → 11 AM | Panchavati Circuit                       | Return to city           | Ram Kund, Sita Gufa, Kalaram Temple, Tapovan |
| 12 PM → 2 PM | Lunch + rest                             |                          |                                              |
| 2 PM → 4 PM  | Saptashrungi Devi Temple                  | 75 km                     | Ropeway ride, hill views                     |
| 6 PM → 8 PM  | Return to Nashik & drop bike             | Total ride ≈ 200 km      |                                              |
</div>

## Day 2 – Sun 30 Nov: Nashik → Shirdi
<div class="day-content">
- 8 AM: MSRTC bus to Shirdi (~3 hrs, ₹250)  
- 11 AM → 4 PM: Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir, Dwarkamai, Gurusthan, Lendi Garden  
- Evening: Relax / shopping at Sai Complex  
- Stay: Sai Ashram / Bhakta Niwas Dharamshala (₹800–900)
</div>

## Day 3 – Mon 1 Dec: Shirdi → Shani Shingnapur → Aurangabad
<div class="day-content">
- 7 AM: Shared cab / MSRTC to Shani Shingnapur (~1.5 hrs)  
- 9 AM → 10 AM: Darshan of Shani Dev  
- 10:30 AM → 2 PM: Travel to Aurangabad (~3–3.5 hrs)  
- Check-in: Budget hotel (₹900–1000)  
- Evening: Explore Bibi Ka Maqbara & Aurangabad Caves
</div>
