Hyderabad → Gujarat Jyotirlinga Circuit Trip (6 Days)

Duration: 1st – 6th October 2025

Route: Secunderabad → Rajkot → Somnath → Dwarka → Okha → Nagpur → Secunderabad

Highlights: Somnath Jyotirlinga, Dwarkadhish Temple, Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, Scenic Gujarat Coastline

Board the train from Secunderabad to Rajkot. Carry snacks, water, charger, and warm clothes. Overnight journey.

Arrive at Rajkot (17:35). Take passenger train (18:20–22:50) to Somnath. Check in at a hotel near the temple (Hotel Sun Plaza or Lords Inn). Rest overnight.

Morning Somnath Darshan (7 AM), followed by local sightseeing: Bhalka Tirth, Triveni Sangam, Gita Mandir, Museum, Surya Mandir, Bhidbhanjan Mahadev, optional Chorwad Beach. Board 23:05 SMNH OKHA Express for Dwarka. Overnight train.

Morning Dwarkadhish Darshan and Gomti Ghat visit. Sightseeing: Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, Rukmini Temple, Bet Dwarka (ferry), Bhadkeshwar Mahadev, Lighthouse, Sudama Setu. Evening transfer to Okha (17:00), board 22939 OKHA BSP Express at 19:05 for Nagpur. Overnight train.

Arrive at Nagpur (20:05). Dinner at station. Board train at 23:10 for Secunderabad. Overnight journey.

Arrive at Secunderabad (07:00). Trip ends — memories begin!
