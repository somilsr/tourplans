Hyderabad → Gujarat Jyotirlinga Circuit Trip (6 Days)

Route: Secunderabad → Rajkot → Somnath → Dwarka → Okha → Nagpur → Secunderabad

Board train from SC to Rajkot. Keep snacks, water, power bank, and warm clothes. Overnight journey.

Arrival at Rajkot, local train to Somnath, hotel check-in. Evening rest.
